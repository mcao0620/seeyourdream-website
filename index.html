See Your Dream

VR Experiences for Pediatric Patients

A 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Organization

About

See Your Dream is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that offers unique and impactful virtual reality (VR) experiences for pediatric patients. Our experiences are designed to alleviate the fear and anxiety associated with many hospital procedures, including MRI scans, surgery, and anesthesia. As a non-profit, we equip hospital staff with both headsets and software as well as training materials to streamline the integration of virtual reality into their day-to-day treatment.

Our Mission

  • to help hospital bound children by providing educational tools and technology that prepare children for medical procedures

  • to foster hope and imagination of children through immersive and interactive technology

  • to support child life specialists and healthcare professionals in alleviating anxiety and improving the hospital experience of children and their families.
